## What to Expect - Your Path to Mental Health

1. **Schedule Your First Therapy Session** - Book online or contact me directly via phone or email
2. **Complete Confidential Intake Forms** - Brief paperwork about your mental health background, symptoms, and therapy goals
3. **Attend Your First Counseling Session** - We'll spend 60 minutes getting to know each other, understanding what brought you to therapy, and creating your treatment plan
4. **Begin Our Therapeutic Work Together** - We'll collaborate on an evidence-based path forward tailored to your mental health needs
